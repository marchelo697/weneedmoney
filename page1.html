Categories of Speaking

Name Description Abbreviation
Original Oratory The traditional persuasively written speech, intended to inform and educate others on a certain topic. OO
Original Prose & Poetry A very different method of speaking involving the use of both poetry and character development to create a story, and intends to send a message to the audience through a multitude of performance elements. OPP
Expository Similarly meant to educate the audience on a certain topic through the use of visual aids and a continuous story board. Expos

Categories of Debate

Name Description Abbreviation
Lincoln-Douglass Modeled after the historical series of debates between Abraham Lincoln and political rival Stephen Douglas, individual competitors debate a resolution with moral implications. Each debate is weighed on a value (a concept such as justice, morality, etc.) and a criterion (an action that helps achieve said value.) This debate is a discussion of ethics and morality.This is a team debate with two people per team. Public forum is a fact-based style where each debate is weighed on a framework (similar to Lincoln Douglas' value.) This type of debate is meant to be accessible to the everyday, or lay, person. LD
