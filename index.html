CHSAA Speech and Debate Mission:

The CHSSA mission in speech and debate is to provide opportunities and assist its participants in developing diverse communication skills while competing at the highest levels in high school speech and debate. The purpose of this is to instill discipline, teamwork, sportsmanship, honesty and integrity in a highly specialized activity. CHSSA Speech and Debate coaches teach their participants to respect the activity's core values, work hard and compete honestly.
